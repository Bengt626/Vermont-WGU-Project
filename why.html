Our Best Seasonal Attractions

See Our Summer Farmers Markets

In our small state of Vermont, there are over 45 different farmers markets to attend each week. No matter where you are in the state, there's sure to be local vendors slinging veggies somewhere near you!

Join Us for Winter!

We love to go outside when it gets cold and the snow comes out. We have events ranging from the Winter Rendezvous in Stowe, to the annual ski jumps, to the Paran Plunge in North Bennington. Have a unique Vermont experience!

The Stowe Winter Carnival

The Winter Carnival is something the residents look forward to putting on each year. There's an ice carving competition, cross-country ski tours, and hot cider welcoming of all.
